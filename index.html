Voices of Left-Behind Children

Some children grew up being strong and resilient, not because they want to, but because they have to. Children with Overseas Filipino Worker (OFW) parents are the children who have to learn how to grow up by themselves; they need to learn from their own. In order to support their children financially, a lot of Filipino parents chose to work abroad to support their families, especially their children. However it results in the challenges that the children face; they struggle academically, socially, and emotionally. Even though the love from their parents is there, their situation will never be easy, because the love they give from a distance will always feel different.
