The Overseas Filipino Workers

Who are the Overseas Filipino
Workers (OFWs)?

Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs) are Filipinos employed abroad under a specific contract and they live abroad for a limited period of time. Many of these workers are the Filipino parents, in order to provide the needs of their families, especially their children. As of now, the Philippines is recognized as one of the largest exporters of overseas workers in the world.




The hard work and courage of OFWs have been recognized countless times, with each of their personal experiences often resonating with others. Every day, they navigate foreign cultures and endure the emotional pain of being separated from their families. However, they are not the only ones facing challenges; their families, especially their children, are also deeply affected by their absence.
